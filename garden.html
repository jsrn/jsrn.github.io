my 2020 garden

like a lot of people, i spent much of 2020 in the garden.

  • removing the seeds from heirloom tomatoes and storing them to grow. easier said than done!
  • taking care of orchids
  • growing thai dragon peppers
  • accidentally decapitating my dahlias!
  • doting on a maranta fascinata (prayer plant)
  • growing many many baby tomatoes
  • putting up the bird bath and solar fountain my mum & sister got me
  • assembling many hanging feeders and filling them with suet, sunflower seeds, etc.
  • watching horton the wood pigeon's daily visits

thank you to my garden & horton the pigeon for improving the dumpster fire that was 2020
